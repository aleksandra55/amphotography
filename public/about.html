About the Photographer

From Modeling to the fashion industry, Aleksandra Mikelovna has been shooting conceptual art, boudoir & fashion editoral around the world,specializing in portrait and conceptual photography with a style consisting of bright and intense imagery. Running a client-friendly, service-oriented business he believes that great creativity often is the result of team effort and values working closely with his clients. Based in Tel Aviv, Aleksandra is ready to create outstanding visuals for you.
